Ruglach Cookies:

ingredients:

  • 1 cup butter, cut up
  • 8 oz cream cheese, softened and cubed
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 cups flour
  • mini chocolate chips

directions:

combine butter, cream cheese, and granulated sugar in bowl of food processor. process until smooth.

add flour and process until mixture starts to leave sides of the bowl and forms a ball. remove from bowl and divide dough into 4 equal portions. shape each portion into a puck.

remove one portion of dough from the refridgerator at a time. lightly flour work surface and rolling pin. place dough and roll into an 8-inch circle, sprinkling chocolate chips. cut circle into strips or triangles. roll up the dough from the outside in. place on lined baking sheets.

bake in preheated 350ÂºF oven for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown. let stand on cookie sheet for a few minutes before removing with spatula. makes about 32.
