Lemon Drop Martini:

ingredients:

  • 2 oz espresso
  • 3/4 oz cointreau or triple sec
  • 1 oz lemon juice
  • 3/4 oz simple syrup
  • sugar for garnish

directions:

combine all ingredients in shaker. shake until ice cold.

wet rim of glass with lemon, dip into sugar to rim. pour into martini glass.
